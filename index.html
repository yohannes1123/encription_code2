About CryptoCipher

This tool provides implementations of various classical cipher algorithms used in cryptography. You can encrypt and decrypt messages using different techniques from simple Caesar cipher to more complex polyalphabetic and transposition ciphers.

How These Ciphers Work

Each cipher uses a different mathematical approach to transform plaintext into ciphertext:

  • Monoalphabetic: Simple substitution of each letter with another
  • Polyalphabetic: Uses multiple substitution alphabets
  • Transposition: Rearranges the letters of the plaintext

Historical Significance

These ciphers have been used throughout history for military and diplomatic communications. While many are now considered insecure for modern applications, they form the foundation of modern cryptography.
